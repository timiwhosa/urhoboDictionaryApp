Urhobo Location and Population

The Urhobo are the major ethnic group in Delta State, one of the thirty six states of the Federal Republic of Nigeria. The Urhobos speak the Urhobo language. The word ‘Urhobo' refers to a group of people rather than a territory. Approximately 6 to 7 million people are Urhobo. They have a social and cultural affinity to the Bini people of Nigeria.

The Urhobo people live in a territory bounded by latitudes 6°and 5°, 15° North and Longitudes 5°, 40° and 6°, 25° East in the Delta and the Bayelsa States of Nigeria. Their neighbors are the Isoko to the southeast, the Itsekiri and Ijaw to the west, the Bini to the north, the Ijaw to the south and the Ukwuani people to the northeast.

Current Problem

The Urhobo language is going into extinction. It’s a sad thing that this language which our forefathers have protected for ages, has so much lost value to this present generation. Majority of our young people cannot speak and communicate in Urhobo. The language is being endangered because in many homes, our children are being spoken to in a language other than Urhobo. In the time of our forefathers, Urhobo was the lingua franca at homes, on the streets, in churches, in schools, in playing grounds, in marketplaces, etc. But that isn’t so today. This ugly situation could be traced to how much we have so embraced westernization at the expense of our mother tongue. If this unpleasant trend continues, it will lead to the extinction of the Urhobo language in the near future. How would you feel if, years to come, you can’t teach your children about your tribe and culture? What would your children learn of your history? Just like the death of a baby is a big blow to its family, the extinction of a language would be a big blow to the owners

How The Urhobo Dictionary App Will Help Solve The Problem

The Urhobo Dictionary App solves the problem by focusing on creating content on the key pillars of learning a language, which are learning, reading, writing, and speaking the Urhobo language with fun and with excitement, from the comfort of users' homes.

Features On What We Are Working On (upcoming App Features)

The Dictionary Section:

We look forward to making sentences and Audio pronunciations of all possible Urhobo Words which are compiled by professionals, qualified writers, translators, and proficient speakers of the Urhobo Language. A 'word' can be searched either by direct typing or by voice search, and that will bring out everything about that word. Since they are different dialects in the Urhobo language, if a word, or a sentence or an audio pronunciation is not correct, the word can be up-voted or voted for by the App users; then they may suggest the correct one, thereby allowing for community contributions — a key metric to growth.

The Learn Section:

Since the primary aim of the app is to aid the learning of the language and since most of the users will be newbies of the language, the App will feature pre-made courses and lessons that will help users of the App learn to speak, read and write basic words and sentences in Urhobo. This is called the Learn Section. It will be arranged into units, modules and lessons. Upon the completion of each module and unit, there will be some simple assessment to test the understanding of users on the module or unit they have just completed. It will feature an audio guide for each word, phrase, or sentence in the lessons which will help users learn to speak better. The lessons will be compiled and structured by experienced and skilled teachers of the Language who have successfully tested their methods and found them to work for even the least beginner of the language.

The Quiz Section:

This is one of the most exciting parts of the App where users can test their knowledge of the language by playing quizzes of different levels and categories; users can compete with friends across the globe and with a global leaderboards of learners where all players are ranked according to their scores. The MVP(current App version) have more than 700 active players of the quiz. This shows users are really interested in it.

The More About Section:

This section exposes you to know more about the Urhobo culture, History, Foods, Dialects, Local Government Areas, Dressing Codes, Kings and Kingdom

How The Urhobo Dictionary App Started

Urhobo dictionary app started when I had just completed my 100 level back in 201. At the time I was undergoing Android Basic Nanodgree by Udacity sponsored by Google and Andela Learning Community; in the course of the program, we built a Miwok app (Sample App for learning the Miwok language). At the end of that Program in October 2018, I decided to work on a real-life project, so I began the journey of creating an App for my local dialect Urhobo, having found out that the language is being endangered. Being someone who has a value for my language and culture led me to this liberation mission — the mission of liberating the Urhobo Language from the pit of extinction.
The first version of the App was released back in December 2019. That took about a year to build, because of my school activities and low resources at the time.
Success Metric and Impact
The Project started as a personal project but now is now made up of a Team of 8 inspired individuals. Since 2019 and 2021, we have done a lot of iterations based on users' feedback, such as recording over 2000 words in Urhobo, the addition of word of the day, and also integrating the Quiz leaderboard which has more than 700 active players.
Using 2021 Analytics we have over 5000 downloads in the Google Play Store, and an active user base of over 2120 and 100 new users on average monthly users, in Nigeria, the United States, the United Kingdom, Germany, Italy, Ghana, Netherlands, Spain, Canada, and others.
