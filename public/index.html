Our Vision

Our vision is to create a platform that will be the best to learn the Urhobo language globally, and also to be an authority to regulate Urhobo Language learning in the nearest future.

About

Urhobo Dictionary App is an app designed to learn the Urhobo language from the comfort of your home with few clicks, fun and excitement . We focus on creating the contents on the basic pillars of learning a language which are writing , speaking, listening and Reading of the language. A person can be able to Speak, read, and read urhobo language to an extent with our current MVP(version).

Features just for you

Dictionary

Contains more than 2000+ words in Urhobo with Audio pronunciations which are compiled by professionals, qualified writers, translators, and proficient speakers of the Urhobo Language

Quiz

This is one of the most exciting parts of the App where users can test their knowledge of the language by playing quizzes of different levels and categories; there is a global leaderboards of learners where all players are ranked according to their scores.

More About Urhobo Section

This section exposes you to know more about the Urhobo culture, History, Foods, Dialects, Local Government Areas, Dressing Codes, Kings, and Kingdom

Success Metric and Impact

The Project started as a personal project but now is now made up of a Team of 8 inspired individuals. Since 2019 and 2021, we have done a lot of iterations based on users' feedback, such as recording over 2000 words in Urhobo, the addition of word of the day, and also integrating the Quiz leaderboard which has more than 700 active players. Using 2021 Analytics we have over 5000 downloads in the Google Play Store, and an active user base of over 2120 and 100 new users on average monthly users, in Nigeria, the United States, the United Kingdom, Germany, Italy, Ghana, Netherlands, Spain, Canada, and others.
